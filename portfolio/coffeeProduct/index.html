Learn the Science behind Roasting

LIGHT ROAST

196°C - 205°C

Lighter-bodied, higher acidity, no obvious roast flavor. This level of roast is ideal for tasting the full origin character of the coffee.

MEDIUM ROAST

210°C - 219°C

Sugar have been further caramelized, and acidity has been muted. This results in coffee with higher body, but some roast flavor imposed.

DARK ROAST

225°C - 245°C

Bittersweet flavors are prominent, aromas and flavors of roast become clearly evident. Littel, if any, origin character remains.
